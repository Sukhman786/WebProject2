Drop Dash Support

How do I track my delivery?
Simply use the tracking ID provided at the time of booking on our main tracking page to see real-time updates of your courier's location.
What are the delivery hours?
Our riders are active from 8:00 AM to 10:00 PM, seven days a week, including public holidays.
Are there weight limits for packages?
Standard bike deliveries can carry up to 10kg. For heavier items, please select the 'Van' option during booking.
How can I pay for the service?
We accept all major credit/debit cards, UPI, and digital wallets directly through our secure payment gateway.
Is insurance included?
Every shipment is covered by basic insurance. For high-value electronics or documents, additional 'Safe-Guard' coverage can be added.
